Captain America

After a failed attempt to enlist during World War II due to his frailty, Steve Rogers volunteers for the Super Soldier Program. The serum enhances him to peak human condition. Captain America becomes the iconic hero of the Allies in WWII and later leads the Avengers in modern times.
