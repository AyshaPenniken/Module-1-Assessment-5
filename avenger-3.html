Hulk

Bruce Banner, a physicist, is exposed to gamma radiation during an experiment gone wrong. The exposure transforms him into the Hulk, a monstrous green being with immense strength. While Banner is brilliant, the Hulk is a destructive force that grows in power with each emotional outburst.
